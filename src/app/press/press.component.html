So what kind of tune is I Can't Wait? A home run. An ebullient rocker, fleshed out and given heft by hints of pathos. A song proceeded by the guitar being plugged into the amp in the best traditions of rock’n’roll, sparking thoughts of the The Manic Street Preacher's punk manifesto Motown Junk, I Can't Wait hits the ground running with full throated rhythm guitar, a propulsive beat and an urgent, impassioned vocal delivery that belies the singer's slight frame. Sweet harmonies in the refrain and jaunty keyboards ensure an effervescent contrast to the verses and thick musical textures.
